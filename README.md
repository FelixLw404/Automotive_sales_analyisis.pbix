# Automotive Sales & Pricing Analysis

Automotive sales and pricing analysis using Power BI, Power Query, and Excel.

## Project Objectives
- Identify pricing gaps and margin opportunities
- Build a stable Top 10 Sellers ranking
- Develop an interactive and business-oriented dashboard

## Dataset
**Source:** Vehicle Sales and Market Trends Dataset (Manheim Market Report)  
**Scope:** Automotive sales transactions  

**Key fields:**
- Seller
- Selling Price
- Market Value (MMR)
- Sale Date
- Vehicle attributes (make, model, year, etc.)

## Tools & Technologies
- **Excel** – Initial data review and validation  
- **Power Query** – ETL (data cleaning, transformations, shaping)  
- **Power BI** – Data modeling, DAX measures, and visualization  

## Key Metrics
- Total Revenue
- Average Selling Price
- Average Market Price (MMR)
- Pricing Gap (%)
- Top 10 Sellers by Revenue
